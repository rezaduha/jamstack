To keep your baseball uniforms looking great, you should know how to clean them. The first step in cleaning baseball uniforms is pre-soaking. Fill a sink with warm water. Add 2 tablespoons of heavy-duty laundry detergent. Choose a brand that contains enzymes and surfactants, such as Tide or Persil. It’s also a good idea to use baking soda to reduce the smell. Soak the uniform for at least an hour before washing. The longer the soaking time, the more likely stains will come out.

Next, consider the price. High-quality fabrics are expensive, so you may want to order extras to make sure you have a back-up for unexpected wear and tear. Many manufacturers outsource manufacturing, which can add delays and mistakes. If you’re looking for a uniform that is both high-quality and affordable, you should choose a direct manufacturer.
