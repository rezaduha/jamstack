2. Use Flutter

Dart is an AOT (Ahead Of Time) accumulated, the client-optimized programming language used to develop apps for IoT devices. Investigating and taking early steps in Flutter and Dart can assist Androids with causing of developing future-prepared mobile applications.

Eventually, what merges an application remaining as user-friendly is its UI/UX design. An Android developer can decide to restrict oneself to simply coding or can be effectively engaged with the design cycle, to really see how a lump of code causes users to feel and experience the application.

4. Adopt Cloud Technologies

The cloud-based technologies will assist you with beating device and operating system fragmentation issues while developing and testing Android apps.

Most enterprises these days allow representatives to get to the business app on their own mobile devices. Thus, the users store both individual and business data on their devices.
