Why should you opt for PGDM instead of MBA?

By Ying Leak Published on May 27, 2022

There is no doubt that Masters in Business Administration, commonly known as MBA, is one of the most favorite choices of career aspirants. This is because the boys and girls see innumerable job prospects opening up in front of them after securing MBA. In addition, those who aspire to start their own business expect to gain entrepreneurial skills with the study of MBA. But over the years, another fresh perspective has emerged and it is sounding much more promising from the career point of view of the aspirants. This is about PGDM which is the Post Graduate Diploma in Management. There are some significant aspects for which many job or business aspirants and even professionals are seeking PGDM and their preference is to study at one of India’s top management schools Thakur Institute of Management Studies and Research (TIMSR).

Is PGDM better than MBA?

PGDM is unique for many of its specialties and it is different from MBA as well. PGDM is a diploma course while MBA is a Masters Degree course. PGDM is a postgraduate program which is offered by IIMs also. Some of the top and reputed universities and business schools provide PGDM. This clearly indicates that when you hold the PGDM degree, your eligibility is much more valued because you have a postgraduate degree in this stream.

It is equally worthwhile knowing that against the MBA program of four years, you spend only two years completing PGDM, thus cutting down 2 years of study. When it comes to recognition, PGDM is as much recognized as MBA. In the corporate sector, both MBA and PGDM are considered at par and so if you have done PGDM, your competence and eligibility is never considered mediocre.

Why pursue PGDM at TIMSR?

You got immense advantages to draw when you study PGDM at an incredible institute, college or business school. In this respect, you don’t have to look elsewhere when you already have a reputed, highly recognized and ISO 9001: 2015 certified MBA College like Thakur Institute of Management Studies & Research. This specially designed program from TIMSR inculcates entrepreneurship forte in you, enhances your critical ability prowess and betters your decision making aptitude. As a consequence of developed abilities completing PGDM at TIMSR, you pick up exceedingly good analytical, logical and critical thinker, which are most essential in business as well job.
