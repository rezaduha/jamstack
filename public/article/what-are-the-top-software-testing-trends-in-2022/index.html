What Are The Top Software Testing Trends In 2022?

By Margaret F. Wise Published on May 27, 2022

The dynamic evolution of technology is affecting an organization in the following terms development, validations, usage, operations, etc. Thus, these organizations are in constant need of innovation and transformation with solutions that optimize regular practices to deliver high-quality software quickly.

Software testing mainly focuses on changes and improvements. It is most important to achieve quality software at speed despite the increase in the complexity of systems, environments, and data.

Here, we are sharing the top trends in software testing services. that have emerged in a few years. With Agile and DevOps, test automation, AI emergence, we are able to see some major transforming trends for 2022.

What Are The Top Software Testing Trends In 2022?

1. Agile and DevOps

Today, several IT firms have adopted Agile considering the dynamically changing shift in requirements. DevOps is a response to the demand for speed.

DevOps includes strict rules, processes, and tools that can assist them in integrating the development and operation activities. This process reduces the time of the processes for development to operations. To be precise, organizations are actually looking for this methodology and other ways to shorten the software development life cycle.

forThis adoption of Agile and DevOps is helping the teams to deliver quality software at reduced time. For the last five years, several businesses have invested in their interests and continue to intensify in the future.

2. Test Automation

To implement DevOps successfully, a software team cannot escape the test automation. It is an integral part of DevOps processes. Businesses are preferring automation testing over manual testing. They are considering keeping most of the regression testing at automation.

Today, there are several automation tools such as Selenium, Katalon, and TestComplete to make automation much more efficient.

We are also seeing the rise in the need for test automation with the API and Services. It is outpacing the functionalities of the end-users on user interfaces.

3. Artificial Intelligence for Testing

Although the application of AI and Machine learning is in its early stage. The AI/ML approaches to address the issues in software testing are not new. The recent growth of AI/ML is posing several opportunities for testing.

The algorithms based on AI/ML generate better test cases, test scripts, test data, and reports. They also render smart analytics and visualization to support the teams to detect faults, understand test coverage, areas of high risk, etc.

4. Test Environments and Data

The rapid growth of the Internet of Things (IoT) simply means more involvement of operating systems from several environments. This poses a challenge for the testing teams to ensure the right scope of test coverage. Indeed, the lack of test environments and data is a top challenge when applying to tests in agile projects.
