Tips for choosing the best mens surf board shorts for you

By Liam Cunningham Published on May 27, 2022

Where do you even start your search when you’re looking for mens surfing t shirts or surf board shorts?

**These tips will help to make your search easier. **

Choose the right material

While canvas and cotton shorts look good and fall really well, these are not suitable for wearing in the water. These fabrics will absorb water and hang heavily on you making it almost impossible to surf. The right material for mens surf board shorts should be stretchy and capable of wicking away water instead of absorbing water. Also look for a fabric that breathes a bit so that it keeps you cool and comfortable instead of hot and uncomfortable. You can even find mens surfing t shirts and shorts that are environmentally friendly and made from recycled fabric.

Check out the seams too

This is something that most people don’t pay much attention to but they can in fact make a huge difference to comfort as well as durability. Surfing is a very active sport so you need to wear clothing that will withstand all the rigors you put it through. Suring shorts and t shirts generally have a very strong seam that is capable of handling anything you put it through. They are also tough enough to handle the damage done by the sun and sea. Good quality surfwear will always have seams that meet these parameters while also being flattened down so that they don’t chafe or irritate your skin.

Make sure you get the right size

Fit matters when you’re out there riding the waves. Shorts that are too big will form ugly gathers at the waist and are more likely to fall off with the weight after absorbing water. Shorts that are too tight on the other hand will make it impossible to bend and turn and will restrict your movements on the board while also being incredibly uncomfortable. Make sure to measure your wait and hips before buying a pair of mens surf board shorts online. If you’re buying mens surfing t shirts online, you’ll need to know your collar size as well as the length.

Is it possible to get the right fit when you buy mens surf board shorts online?

Absolutely! All stores that sell mens surf board shorts and mens surfing t shirts online will mention all relevant details from the material used to the sizes each item is available in. Once you know what you’re looking for you can filter down your options to find the best choices for you. You can further filter down the prices so that you find one that fits your budget too.

One piece of advice that most surfing experts offer is to shop the big brands when you’re looking for mens surf board shorts. These brands may cost a little more but you get what you pay for. The materials they use are more superior and they think of every little thing from the types of seams to the pockets and other styling detail.
